shyMeets App

shyMeets is an exceptional app that simplifies event discovery in your city by categorizing upcoming events by type, including music concerts, art exhibitions, theater performances, and sports matches. Its user-friendly interface, comprehensive event details, and ensure users stay connected to the vibrant cultural scene, making it the go-to app for staying informed and engaged with local events.

The following technologies were used to create this app

  • React
  • AWS Lambda
  • Google OAuth
  • Jest
  • Puppeteer
